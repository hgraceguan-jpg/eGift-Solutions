6 Key Insights & Decision Factors
💰
Cost

Pluxee Physical (PHP 32,480) is the cheapest option, saving PHP 880 over Giftaway. Pluxee Digital (PHP 40,640) has a higher upfront cost due to the PHP 8,000 one-time Portal Access Fee — once paid, the portal remains fully accessible at no additional recurring cost. Giftaway (PHP 33,360) offers the best value for digital-only distribution, with its PHP 50,000 setup fee currently waived (may be enforced in future).

⚡
Delivery Speed

Giftaway is the fastest — credits are released immediately upon payment with digital delivery to email or mobile. Both Pluxee options require 3–5 calendar days lead time.

⚙️
Technology

Giftaway offers the most advanced platform features: API integration, bulk file upload, and web dashboard. Neither Pluxee option mentions API or bulk upload capabilities in the submitted documents.

📋
Agreement Protection

Giftaway provides a comprehensive 36-month Enterprise Agreement with explicit clauses for data privacy, late payment, force majeure, and dispute resolution. Pluxee quotations are transactional with limited long-term terms.

🏷️
Tax Treatment

Giftaway credits are VAT Exempt and not subject to EWT per BIR ruling — a significant compliance advantage. Pluxee quotations do not address VAT exemption on the gift value. Note: Pluxee documents are not valid for input tax claims.

🎁
Credit Validity

Giftaway eGifts are valid for 365 days once converted. Pluxee does not specify credit or card validity periods in the submitted documents.
